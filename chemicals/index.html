Stability and Reactivity

  • Reactivity - none known.
  • Chemical Stability - stable under normal conditions.
  • Hazardous polymerisation - hazardous polymerisation does not occur.
  • Hazardous reactions - none under normal processing.
  • Conditions to avoid - avoid dust formation. Incompatible products. Excess heat.
  • Incompatible materials - Strong oxidising agents. Strong acids. Floruine. Metals.
  • Hazardous decomposition products - sodium oxides.
